Chocolate Chip Cookies

Prep Time:15 minutes

Cook Time:10 minutes

Steps
  1. Preheat The Oven to 350 oF(180 oC)
  2. Mix Flour sugar in a bowl.
  3. Add Chocolate Chips and Stir well
  4. Bake for 10 minutes or until Golden Browns
 
    Ingredients
Ingredients Quality
Flow 2 Cups
sugar 1 Cups
Chocolate Chips 1 Cups
